Ragu a'la Bolognese

By Chef Miko≈Çaj

Prep time: 2h

Ingredients:

  • 300g Minced Beef
  • 200g Minced Pork
  • 100g Pork Belly
  • 700g Plain Passata
  • 2 medium sized carrots
  • 4 celery sticks
  • 1 Large red onion
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • Oregano, Fresh Basil, Salt, Pepper
  • large glass of Dry Red Wine

Preparation:

  1. Blend the vegetables together into a sofritto.
  2. Render the fat out from the bacon on low heat nad fry the bacon in it.
  3. Once the bacon is fried, pour in the sofritto.
  4. Once the Sofritto has changed color, throw in the meat.
  5. Turn the heat to medium high and fry the meat while aggresively cutting it with your spatula.
  6. Once the meat is done pour in the wine and let it evaporate.
  7. Once the wine has evaporated put in the tomato paste and Passata.
  8. Add Oregano, Basil leaves, Salt, Pepper to taste.
  9. Let the sauce simmer on low heat for at least 2 hours, the longer the better
  10. Enjoy with an egg tagletelle and a dash of pecorino cheese.
